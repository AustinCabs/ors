More About Us

When visiting Koronadal City, you'll feel right at home at FB Hotel and Convention Center, which offers quality accommodation and great service. Only 2.6 km from the city center, the hotel's strategic location ensures that guests can quickly and easily reach many local points of interest. At FB Hotel and Convention Center, the excellent service and superior facilities make for an unforgettable stay. To name a few of the hotel's facilities, there are free Wi-Fi in all rooms, 24-hour security, car power charging station, daily housekeeping, wheelchair accessible.
